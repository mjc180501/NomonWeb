How to Play: Where's Waldo Clock Game

Step 1 of 4
Step 1: Meet the Character
This is the character you'll be looking for in the game. He'll be somewhere on the screen with a yellow glow around him.
In the actual game, you need to find this character and identify which clock is closest to him.
Step 2: Understanding the Clocks
The clocks have hands that rotate. Watch this clock - the hand spins around continuously.
Press SPACE when the hand points straight UP (12 o'clock)
Timing is important! The game learns your reaction time during calibration.
Step 3: How to Select a Clock
To select a clock, you press SPACE when its hand points UP. Try it with this clock!
Press SPACE when the GREEN clock's hand points UP!
In the game, multiple clocks will be spinning. You need to select the one closest to the character.
Step 4: Game Flow
Here's what happens in the game:
  1. Calibration (3 rounds): A TARGET clock is highlighted. Practice timing with that clock.
  2. Game (5 rounds): Find the character and select the closest clock.
  3. Scoring: Get +10 points for correct selections. Build streaks!
  4. Feedback: At the end, tell us if the system made mistakes.
Remember:
  • Press SPACE when the clock hand points UP
  • Look for the yellow glow around the character
  • Choose the clock that's closest to the character
